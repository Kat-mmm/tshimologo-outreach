About us

Our Mission, Vision, and Values

Our Mission

At Tshimologo Outreach, we are on a mission to bridge the information gap for high school learners in rural and township communities across South Africa. By connecting them with educational and career resources, we equip them with the tools and self-belief needed to shape a successful future.

Our Vision

Our vision is a South Africa where every rural learner has equal access to career guidance and opportunities. By empowering these youth, we aim to drive community progress and create a brighter future for all.

Our Core Values

Guiding principles that shape our commitment to empowering youth, fostering growth, and creating lasting impact in every community we serve.

Trust

Building trust in every relationship we form with learners, partners, and communities.

Growth

Committed to lifelong learning, for both individuals and the organization.

Teamwork

Believing in collaboration and mutual support to achieve the best results.

People & Relationships

Placing people and relationships at the heart of everything we do.

Innovation

Encouraging creativity and embracing change to keep evolving in our outreach.
